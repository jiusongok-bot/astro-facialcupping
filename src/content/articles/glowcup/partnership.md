We work with select brands and practitioners who share our commitment to quality and transparency. Partnership with GlowCup is not for sale — it is earned through demonstrated alignment with our standards.

## Partnership Criteria

Potential partners must demonstrate medical-grade material standards, transparent manufacturing processes, and a commitment to consumer safety. We prioritize brands that provide clear product information, stand behind their products with reasonable guarantees, and engage honestly with customer feedback. Brands that use misleading marketing, exaggerate claims, or avoid disclosing material sourcing are not a fit.

## How to Reach Us

For partnership inquiries, please use the contact form on our site. Include your brand name, product category, and a brief description of why you believe there is alignment. We review inquiries quarterly and will follow up if there is mutual interest. Due to volume, we cannot personally respond to every submission, but all inquiries are read and considered.
