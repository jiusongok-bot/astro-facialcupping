We believe in honest, evidence-based information.

![Wellness therapy session](/images/articles/therapy-treatment.jpg)

## Evidence First

We present what the research says, what it doesn't say, and where the evidence is limited. We don't overpromise.

## Transparency

When we recommend tools, we disclose our evaluation methodology. We do not accept payment for editorial placements.

## Accessibility

Facial cupping information should be accessible to everyone. Our content is written for beginners while remaining useful for experienced practitioners.
