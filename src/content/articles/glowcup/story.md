GlowCup started with a simple observation: people searching for facial cupping information online had few reliable, well-researched resources.

![Facial cupping product](/images/articles/woman-cupping-cup.jpg)

## The Beginning

We set out to create a comprehensive resource center that covers facial cupping from every angle — benefits, techniques, safety, tools, and research.

## Our Role

GlowCup evaluates facial cupping tools based on material quality, usability and safety standards. We operate FacialCupping.com as an independent editorial resource.
