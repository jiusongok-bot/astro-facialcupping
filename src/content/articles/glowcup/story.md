GlowCup started with a simple observation: people searching for facial cupping information online had few reliable, well-researched resources. Most search results were either thin blog posts promoting affiliate products or dense academic articles inaccessible to general readers. There was no middle ground — no place that combined thorough research with practical, readable guidance.

## The Beginning

We set out to create a comprehensive resource center covering facial cupping from every angle — benefits, techniques, safety, tools, and research. Our team includes contributors with backgrounds in skincare, research methodology, and content creation who share a commitment to honest information. We spent months reviewing the existing literature, testing tools, and consulting practitioners before publishing a single article.

The first version of the site launched in early 2026 with 30 articles across 8 topic areas. Since then we have expanded to 53 articles across 12 sections, added detailed product evaluations, and developed a structured review process for all health-related content. Every article on the site has been written, reviewed, and edited with the same editorial standards.

## Our Approach to Tool Evaluation

GlowCup evaluates facial cupping tools based on material quality, usability, and safety standards. We purchase products ourselves for testing rather than relying on manufacturer samples. Each tool is assessed for silicone grade (medical-grade or food-grade), suction control precision, edge finishing quality, durability through repeated use, and ease of cleaning. Our evaluation criteria are published on our Product Standards page so readers understand exactly how we reach our recommendations.

## Independence

We operate FacialCupping.com as an independent editorial resource — our content is not influenced by manufacturers, retailers, or advertisers. Every recommendation on this site is the result of our own evaluation process, and we clearly label any affiliate relationships when they exist. We do not accept payment for editorial placements, and no brand can purchase a positive review or guaranteed placement.

## Looking Forward

We continue to expand the site with new articles, updated research summaries, and improved tool evaluations as the field evolves. Our research section is updated monthly to reflect new publications. We are working on adding original before-and-after documentation, video technique guides, and more detailed product comparisons. The site will always be a work in progress because the field of facial cupping research is still developing, and we are committed to keeping pace with new evidence.
