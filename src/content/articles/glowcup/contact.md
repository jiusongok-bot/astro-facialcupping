We welcome questions, feedback, and partnership inquiries from our readers. Here is how to reach the right team member quickly.

## General Inquiries

For general questions about facial cupping — techniques, benefits, safety, or tool selection — check our comprehensive guides and FAQ sections first. We have built the site to answer the most common questions in depth, and you will likely find your answer there faster than waiting for a reply.

## Business Inquiries

For partnerships, press, or business questions, please email us through the contact form. Specify the nature of your inquiry in the subject line so we can direct it to the appropriate person. We review business inquiries on a rolling basis.

## Response Time

We typically respond to inquiries within 2-3 business days. If you have not heard back within five business days, please resend your message as it may have been caught by our spam filter. We value your time and strive to provide thoughtful responses rather than rushed replies.
