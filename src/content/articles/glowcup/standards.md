Our product evaluation framework is built on three pillars.

## Material Quality

We verify material grade, check for certifications, and assess durability over time.

## Usability

We evaluate cup design, suction control, ease of use for beginners, and ergonomic considerations.

## Safety

We assess whether products meet safety standards for skin contact, including appropriate suction levels for facial use.
