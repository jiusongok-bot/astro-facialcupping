Consistency is key with facial cupping. This 5-minute routine can be incorporated into your morning or evening skincare.

## Morning Routine (5 minutes)

1. Cleanse face
2. Apply facial oil
3. Neck drainage (1 minute)
4. Cheeks and jawline (2 minutes)
5. Forehead (1 minute)
6. Under-eye area with smallest cup (1 minute)

## Frequency

Start with 3 times per week and gradually increase to daily as your skin adapts.
