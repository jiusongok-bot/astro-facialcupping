Follow this step-by-step guide for a complete facial cupping routine.

![Facial cupping massage](/images/articles/cupping-massage.jpg)

## Step 1: Prepare

Cleanse your face and apply a generous layer of facial oil. This creates the glide needed for smooth cup movement.

## Step 2: Neck and Jaw

Start at the base of the neck, using upward strokes toward the jawline. Then work along the jaw from chin to ear.

## Step 3: Cheeks

Use the medium cup, moving from the nose outward toward the ears in sweeping motions.

## Step 4: Forehead

Use the largest cup, moving from the center of the forehead outward and upward.
