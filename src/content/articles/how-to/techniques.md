Different techniques produce different effects. Here's a reference guide.

![Cupping therapy technique](/images/articles/therapist-patient.jpg)

## Gliding Technique

Keep the cup in constant motion across the skin. Best for lymphatic drainage and reducing puffiness.

## Flash Cupping

Quickly apply and release the cup in rapid succession. This stimulates blood flow without sustained suction.

## Stationary (Light)

Place the cup with very light suction for 3-5 seconds only. Use on specific points of tension. Not recommended for beginners.
