Avoid these common mistakes to get the best results from facial cupping.

## Too Much Suction

The number one mistake. Facial skin is delicate — use the lightest suction that still creates a seal.

## Skipping Oil

Without adequate lubrication, cups drag on the skin and can cause irritation or bruising.

## Staying in One Spot

Keeping a cup stationary increases the risk of bruising. Always keep cups moving.

## Using Body Cups on the Face

Body cupping sets are too large and create too much suction for facial use. Always use cups specifically designed for the face.
