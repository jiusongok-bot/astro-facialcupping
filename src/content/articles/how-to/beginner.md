Starting facial cupping for the first time? This guide covers everything you need to know.

![Facial cupping set equipment](/images/articles/cupping-cups-set.jpg)

## What You'll Need

- A facial cupping set (silicone cups recommended for beginners)
- A facial oil or serum for glide
- Clean, dry skin

## First Session Tips

Start with the largest cup on the cheeks and forehead. Use very light suction — you should feel a gentle pull, not pain. Keep the cup moving at all times.
