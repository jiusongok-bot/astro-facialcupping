Facial cupping therapy applies controlled suction to the skin of the face using specialized cups. This technique is used by estheticians, acupuncturists, and wellness practitioners worldwide.

![Facial cupping therapy session](/images/articles/therapy-treatment.jpg)

## Therapeutic Applications

Practitioners use facial cupping to address concerns including fluid retention, dull complexion, tension in the jaw and forehead, and signs of aging.

## Session Structure

A typical facial cupping session lasts 15-30 minutes. The practitioner applies oil to the face, then uses cups in specific patterns following lymphatic pathways and muscle groups.
