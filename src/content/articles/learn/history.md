Cupping therapy has been practiced for over 3,000 years across multiple civilizations.

![Traditional massage therapy](/images/articles/massage-therapy.jpg)

## Ancient Origins

The earliest recorded use of cupping dates to ancient Egypt, where it was documented in the Ebers Papyrus around 1550 BCE. Traditional Chinese Medicine adopted cupping as a core modality, and the practice also appeared in ancient Greek, Roman, and Middle Eastern medicine.

## Modern Evolution

Facial cupping is a relatively recent adaptation. As interest in non-invasive beauty treatments grew in the 2010s, practitioners began adapting traditional cupping techniques specifically for facial use, using softer materials and gentler suction.
