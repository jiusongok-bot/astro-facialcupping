## Is facial cupping painful?

No. Facial cupping should feel like a gentle pulling sensation. If you experience pain, the suction is too strong.

## Does facial cupping leave marks?

Unlike body cupping, proper facial cupping should not leave marks. The cups are kept in constant motion with light suction.

## How often should I do facial cupping?

Most practitioners recommend starting with 2-3 sessions per week, each lasting 5-10 minutes.

## Can I do facial cupping at home?

Yes. Many facial cupping sets are designed for home use. Start with gentle suction and short sessions.
