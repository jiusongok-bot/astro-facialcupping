Every piece of content on FacialCupping.com goes through a structured review process.

## Research Phase

We review available scientific literature, expert opinions, and practitioner experience.

## Writing Phase

Content is written with clear structure, cited sources, and balanced perspective.

## Review Phase

All health-related content is reviewed for accuracy and completeness before publication.

## Update Phase

Published content is reviewed periodically and updated as new evidence becomes available.
