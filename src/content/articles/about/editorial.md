All content on FacialCupping.com follows these editorial principles.

## Accuracy

We fact-check all health claims and cite sources where available. When evidence is limited, we say so explicitly.

## Independence

Editorial content is not influenced by commercial partnerships. Our tool recommendations are based on evaluation criteria, not sponsorship.

## Accessibility

We write in clear, plain language. Technical terms are explained. Content is structured for easy scanning.

## Updates

We review and update content regularly to reflect new research and best practices.
