FacialCupping.com exists to be the most comprehensive and trustworthy resource for facial cupping information online. We built this site because the existing options were not good enough.

## Why We Built the Site

Facial cupping is growing in popularity, but quality information is hard to find. Many sources are either promotional pieces designed to sell products or dismissive articles that brush off the practice without serious examination. We aim for the middle ground: honest, well-researched content that helps people make informed decisions about whether and how to incorporate facial cupping into their routine.

The result is over 50 articles covering benefits, techniques, safety, tools, research, brand comparisons, and more — all written with the same standard of editorial independence. We do not accept payment for editorial content, and all product evaluations follow the same criteria regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship.

## What We Cover

Our content is organized into 12 topic areas. The Learn section covers the fundamentals — what facial cupping is, how it works, and its history. Benefits dives into specific outcomes like lymphatic drainage, circulation, jawline contouring, and wrinkle reduction. How-To provides step-by-step guidance for beginners through advanced practitioners. Safety addresses contraindications, side effects, and proper technique. Research presents the available clinical evidence with transparent assessments of its strengths and limitations. Tools and Brands evaluate products independently. Comparisons helps readers choose between facial cupping and alternative methods like gua sha and microcurrent.

## Our Commitment

We commit to evidence-based content, transparent methodology, and independence from commercial influence in our editorial decisions. When we do not know something, we say so. When the evidence is limited, we acknowledge it. When a product is genuinely good, we recommend it regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship.

We update our content regularly to reflect new research and reader feedback. This site is a living resource, not a static publication. Every article displays its publication date and last updated date, and we track new studies monthly to keep our research summaries current.

## How We Are Different

Unlike many skincare and wellness sites, we do not use affiliate marketing as our primary business model. Our product evaluations are based on material quality, safety testing, and usability criteria — not commission rates. We clearly label any affiliate relationships when they exist. We also go further than most sites in acknowledging the limits of available evidence rather than overpromising results.
