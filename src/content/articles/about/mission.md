FacialCupping.com exists to be the most comprehensive and trustworthy resource for facial cupping information online. We built this site because the existing options were not good enough — and we believe anyone exploring facial cupping deserves access to honest, well-researched, evidence-based content.

## Why We Built a Trusted Facial Cupping Resource

Facial cupping is growing in popularity, but quality information is hard to find. Many sources are either promotional pieces designed to sell products or dismissive articles that brush off the practice without serious examination. We aim for the middle ground: honest, well-researched content that helps people make informed decisions about whether and how to incorporate facial cupping into their routine.

Our [editorial standards](/about/editorial) are built on transparency and independence. Every article is evaluated against the same criteria, regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship with a brand. We do not accept payment for editorial content, and our [review process](/about/review-process) applies uniform rigor across every product and technique we cover.

## What Our Facial Cupping Content Covers

The result is over 50 articles covering benefits, techniques, safety, tools, research, brand comparisons, and more. Our content is organized into 12 topic areas:

The [Learn](/learn) section covers the fundamentals — what facial cupping is, how it works, and its history. [Benefits](/benefits) dives into specific outcomes like lymphatic drainage, circulation, jawline contouring, and wrinkle reduction. [How-To](/how-to) provides step-by-step guidance for beginners through advanced practitioners. [Safety](/safety) addresses contraindications, side effects, and proper technique. [Research](/research) presents the available clinical evidence with transparent assessments of its strengths and limitations. [Tools and Brands](/tools) evaluate products independently. [Comparisons](/comparisons) helps readers choose between facial cupping and alternative methods like gua sha and microcurrent.

## Who We Serve

Our readers include beginners curious about facial cupping for the first time, experienced practitioners looking for deeper technical guidance, and skincare professionals seeking evidence-based information to share with clients. Whether you are researching your first cup set or refining your technique after years of practice, our goal is the same: to provide reliable, accessible information that respects your intelligence and supports your decisions.

## Our Commitment to Evidence-Based Information

We commit to evidence-based content, transparent methodology, and independence from commercial influence in our editorial decisions. When we do not know something, we say so. When the evidence is limited, we acknowledge it. When a product is genuinely good, we recommend it regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship.

Our editorial process includes fact-checking against published research and [transparent disclosure](/about/editorial) of what we know and what we do not. We track new clinical studies monthly to keep our research summaries current — for example, published reviews such as those indexed in [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=facial+cupping) help us stay on top of emerging evidence.

We update our content regularly to reflect new research and reader feedback. This site is a living resource, not a static publication. Every article displays its publication date and last updated date. If you have corrections, suggestions, or questions, our [contact page](/about/contact) is always open.

## How We Are Different from Other Skincare Websites

Unlike many skincare and wellness sites, we do not use affiliate marketing as our primary business model. Our product evaluations are based on material quality, safety testing, and usability criteria — not commission rates. We clearly label any affiliate relationships when they exist. We also go further than most sites in acknowledging the limits of available evidence rather than overpromising results.
