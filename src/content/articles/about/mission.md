FacialCupping.com exists to be the most comprehensive and trustworthy resource for facial cupping information online.

![Professional medical expertise](/images/articles/doctor-smiling.jpg)

## Why It Matters

Facial cupping is growing in popularity, but quality information is hard to find. Many sources are either promotional or dismissive. We aim for the middle ground: honest, well-researched content that helps people make informed decisions.

## Our Commitment

We commit to evidence-based content, transparent methodology, and independence from commercial influence in our editorial decisions.
