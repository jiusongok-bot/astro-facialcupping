FacialCupping.com exists to be the most comprehensive and trustworthy resource for facial cupping information online. We built this site because the existing options were not good enough.

![Professional medical expertise](/images/articles/doctor-smiling.jpg)

## Why We Built the Site

Facial cupping is growing in popularity, but quality information is hard to find. Many sources are either promotional pieces designed to sell products or dismissive articles that dismiss the practice without serious examination. We aim for the middle ground: honest, well-researched content that helps people make informed decisions about whether and how to incorporate facial cupping into their routine.

The result is hundreds of pages covering benefits, techniques, safety, tools, research, brand comparisons, and more — all written with the same standard of editorial independence.

## Our Commitment

We commit to evidence-based content, transparent methodology, and independence from commercial influence in our editorial decisions. When we do not know something, we say so. When the evidence is limited, we acknowledge it. When a product is genuinely good, we recommend it regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship.

We update our content regularly to reflect new research and reader feedback. This site is a living resource, not a static publication.
