We would love to hear from you. Whether you have a content correction, a question about facial cupping, or a business proposal, this page explains how to reach the right person and what to expect.

## How to Reach Us

**Email:** [contact@facialcupping.com](mailto:contact@facialcupping.com)

For the fastest response, send an email to the address above. Include the specific page URL and a clear description of your inquiry so we can route it to the appropriate team member. We monitor this inbox Monday through Friday and typically reply within **2 business days**.

## Content Feedback

If you have found an error, broken link, or outdated information on any page, please let us know. We are committed to accuracy and appreciate readers who help us maintain high standards. When reporting an issue, include the page URL, the section or paragraph in question, and any supporting source that confirms the correction.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond within 2 business days. If you have not heard back within 5 business days, please resend your message — it may have been caught by our spam filter.

### Do you offer medical or dermatological advice?

No. FacialCupping.com provides educational content only. We do not diagnose conditions or prescribe treatments. For personalized medical advice, consult a licensed dermatologist or healthcare provider.

### Can I contribute an article or guest post?

We occasionally accept expert contributions. If you are a licensed esthetician, dermatologist, or Traditional Chinese Medicine practitioner with original insights, send a brief pitch to our email above. We review all submissions for accuracy and alignment with our editorial standards.

### Do you sell products directly?

No. We review and recommend facial cupping tools and oils, but we do not sell products directly. Our recommendations are based on independent research and testing.
