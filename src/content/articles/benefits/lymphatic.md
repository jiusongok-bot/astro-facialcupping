The lymphatic system is a network of vessels and nodes that helps remove waste and excess fluid from tissues.

![Cupping therapy on arm](/images/articles/cupping-arm.jpg)

## Facial Lymphatic Pathways

The face has a rich network of lymphatic vessels. Cupping movements that follow these pathways — generally from the center of the face outward and downward toward the neck — can support natural drainage.

## Signs Your Lymphatic System Needs Support

Persistent puffiness, dull skin, and a feeling of heaviness in the face may indicate sluggish lymphatic flow.
