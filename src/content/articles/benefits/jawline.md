Facial cupping can help define the jawline by reducing fluid retention and releasing tension in the masseter muscles.

## How It Works for Jawline

Cupping movements along the jawline encourage lymphatic drainage, which can reduce puffiness. For people who clench their jaw, the gentle suction may also help release muscle tension.

## Technique Tips

Use the smaller cups along the jawline, moving from the center of the chin outward toward the ear. Apply light to medium suction and keep the cup moving.
