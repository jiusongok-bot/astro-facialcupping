Facial cupping is reported to offer a range of benefits for skin health and overall wellbeing.

![Jade roller skincare products](/images/articles/jade-roller-skincare.jpg)

## Key Reported Benefits

- Reduced facial puffiness through lymphatic drainage
- Improved blood circulation and skin radiance
- Potential reduction in fine lines and wrinkles
- Relief from facial tension and jaw tightness
- Enhanced product absorption when combined with serums

## What the Research Says

While many users report positive experiences, clinical research specific to facial cupping is still limited. Most evidence comes from broader cupping therapy studies and anecdotal reports.
