Facial puffiness is often caused by fluid accumulation during sleep. Facial cupping is particularly effective at addressing this concern.

## Why It Works

The suction and movement of the cups physically push stagnant fluid through lymphatic vessels toward lymph nodes, where it can be processed and eliminated.

## Morning Routine

A 5-minute facial cupping session in the morning can significantly reduce puffiness, particularly around the eyes and cheeks.
