One of the most popular reasons people try facial cupping is its potential to reduce the appearance of wrinkles.

## The Theory

By stimulating blood flow and encouraging collagen production, facial cupping may help plump the skin from within, reducing the visibility of fine lines.

## Realistic Expectations

Facial cupping is not a replacement for proven anti-aging treatments like retinoids or professional procedures. It works best as a complementary practice within a broader skincare routine.
