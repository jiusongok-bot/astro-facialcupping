Blood circulation is essential for healthy skin. Facial cupping creates a localized increase in blood flow that can benefit skin health.

![Cupping therapy for circulation](/images/articles/cupping-shoulder.jpg)

## Benefits of Better Circulation

Increased blood flow delivers more oxygen and nutrients to skin cells, supports cell turnover, and can give the skin a natural, healthy glow.

## Visible Effects

Regular facial cupping practitioners often report improved skin tone and a more radiant complexion within a few weeks of consistent practice.
