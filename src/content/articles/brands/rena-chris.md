Rena Chris has become a popular choice on Amazon for affordable facial cupping sets. But does lower price mean lower quality?

![Facial cupping sets](/images/articles/cupping-cups-set.jpg)

## What You Get

The Rena Chris facial cupping set typically includes 4 silicone cups in different sizes, a storage pouch, and an instruction guide. The cups are made from silicone that feels comparable to more expensive brands.

## Build Quality

The silicone used by Rena Chris is food-grade and BPA-free. While not certified medical-grade like some premium brands, the quality is adequate for home use. The cups have a smooth finish and consistent wall thickness.

## Suction Performance

The cups create a reliable seal and maintain suction well. The different sizes allow for targeted treatment of various face areas. The largest cup works well for cheeks and forehead, while the smallest is appropriate for under-eye area.

## Value Proposition

At a fraction of the price of premium brands, the Rena Chris set offers good value for beginners who want to try facial cupping without a significant investment.

## Verdict

The Rena Chris facial cupping set is an excellent entry-level option. While it may not match the material certification of premium brands, the practical performance is solid. For most home users, this set provides everything needed to start a facial cupping practice.
