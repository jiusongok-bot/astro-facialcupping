Primally Pure is a clean beauty brand known for its natural, non-toxic skincare philosophy. Their facial cupping set has gained popularity among those who prioritize chemical-free beauty tools and want a complete system that includes both cups and compatible skincare products.

## What Primally Pure Offers

Primally Pure's facial cupping set includes medical-grade silicone cups in multiple sizes along with their signature organic facial oil. What sets them apart from most competitors is that the set is designed as a complete system — the oil is formulated specifically to provide the right glide consistency for cupping while delivering skincare benefits through ingredients like jojoba, rosehip, and sea buckthorn. The set comes in a reusable storage case with an illustrated guide.

## Material Quality

The cups are made from platinum-grade silicone — the highest standard for skin-contact materials, exceeding even standard medical-grade requirements. They are free from BPA, phthalates, latex, and other common irritants. The cups feel soft and flexible, appropriate for facial use, with smooth edge finishing that prevents dragging. In our testing, the cups maintained their shape and suction performance through repeated use without any material breakdown.

## The Complete Experience

Primally Pure positions their cupping set as part of a holistic facial massage practice rather than just a tool purchase. They provide detailed written and video guides on technique and recommend pairing the cups with their facial oils for optimal glide. The facial oil included with the set is a blend of organic jojoba, rosehip, seabuckthorn, and vitamin E — ingredients chosen for both slip and skin-nourishing properties. This integrated approach is appealing for users who want a curated experience without researching separate products.

## Pricing and Value

The Primally Pure facial cupping set is priced at a premium ($40-55) compared to generic Amazon options. The value proposition hinges on three factors: the platinum-grade silicone quality (superior to most competitors), the inclusion of their high-quality organic facial oil, and the brand's clean beauty reputation. If these factors matter to you, the price is reasonable. If you already have a preferred facial oil and are primarily looking for quality cups, you can find similar silicone quality at a lower price from other brands.

## Verdict

Primally Pure offers a quality facial cupping set for those who value clean beauty and are willing to invest in a premium brand. The silicone quality is excellent, the integrated oil-and-cup system is well-designed, and the educational resources are helpful for beginners. However, similar quality silicone cups can be found at lower prices if brand reputation is not a priority. This set is best suited for users who want a complete, curated system rather than assembling components separately.
