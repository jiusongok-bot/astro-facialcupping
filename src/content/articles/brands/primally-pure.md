Primally Pure is a clean beauty brand known for its natural, non-toxic skincare philosophy. Their facial cupping set has gained popularity among those who prioritize chemical-free beauty tools.

![Natural skincare products](/images/articles/skincare-cream.jpg)

## What Primally Pure Offers

Primally Pure's facial cupping set includes medical-grade silicone cups in multiple sizes. What sets them apart is their commitment to pairing their cupping tools with their signature organic facial oils.

## Material Quality

The cups are made from platinum-grade silicone — the highest standard for skin-contact materials. They are free from BPA, phthalates, and latex. The cups feel soft and flexible, appropriate for facial use.

## The Complete Experience

Primally Pure positions their cupping set as part of a holistic facial massage practice. They provide detailed guides on technique and recommend pairing the cups with their facial oils for optimal glide and skin benefits.

## Pricing and Value

The Primally Pure facial cupping set is priced at a premium compared to generic options. You are paying for the brand's clean beauty reputation, high-quality materials, and educational resources.

## Verdict

Primally Pure offers a quality facial cupping set for those who value clean beauty and are willing to invest in a premium brand. The silicone quality is excellent, and the accompanying education resources are helpful for beginners. However, similar quality silicone cups can be found at lower prices if brand reputation is not a priority.
