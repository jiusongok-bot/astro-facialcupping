Bellabaci is one of the most recognized brands in the facial cupping space, known for their distinctive pink silicone cups and comprehensive treatment systems. Founded by a South African esthetician, the brand has built a global presence through a combination of product quality and practitioner education.

## Product Range

Bellabaci offers several facial cupping sets, from basic starter kits (3 cups with a guide) to complete treatment systems that include multiple cup sizes, training oils, and access to video libraries. Their cups feature a patented design with a built-in squeeze-and-release suction mechanism that gives users fine control over pressure. The range includes five cup sizes — extra small through large — covering the entire face from the delicate under-eye area to the broader cheeks and forehead.

## Design and Quality

Bellabaci cups are made from medical-grade silicone (USP Class VI), which is the highest standard for skin-contact materials. The design includes a textured grip area that makes it easy to control suction even with oily hands. The cups maintain consistent wall thickness and have smoothly finished edges that glide without dragging. In our testing, the cups showed no signs of material degradation after 30+ uses with proper cleaning. The silicone remains flexible and odorless over time, unlike cheaper alternatives that may stiffen or develop odors.

## Training and Support

One of Bellabaci's standout features is their emphasis on education. Each set includes access to video tutorials and illustrated guides covering basic techniques, lymphatic drainage sequences, and safety precautions. They also offer professional training courses for estheticians who want to add facial cupping to their service menu. This educational component adds significant value, especially for beginners who might otherwise develop poor technique habits.

## Pricing

Bellabaci sets are mid-range to premium priced. The basic starter kit is affordable (around $25-35), while the complete treatment system represents a more significant investment ($60-80). Given the medical-grade material quality and the included educational resources, the pricing is reasonable compared to premium skincare tools of similar quality. The cups are also sold individually for replacement, which extends the usable life of the set.

## Verdict

Bellabaci is a solid choice for both beginners and professionals. The cup quality is excellent across all sizes, the educational resources make it easier to learn proper technique, and the medical-grade silicone provides peace of mind for regular use. If you want a brand with a proven track record in the facial cupping space, Bellabaci is worth considering. The main drawback is the premium price compared to generic silicone sets, though the difference in material quality and support justifies the cost for most users.
