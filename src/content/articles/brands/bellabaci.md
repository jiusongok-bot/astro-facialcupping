Bellabaci is one of the most recognized brands in the facial cupping space, known for their distinctive pink silicone cups and comprehensive treatment systems.

![Massage oil and treatment](/images/articles/massage-oil.jpg)

## Product Range

Bellabaci offers several facial cupping sets, from basic starter kits to complete treatment systems that include multiple cup sizes, oils, and training materials. Their cups feature a unique patented design with a built-in suction mechanism.

## Design and Quality

Bellabaci cups are made from medical-grade silicone. The design includes a textured grip area that makes it easy to control suction. The cups are available in three sizes to target different areas of the face.

## Training and Support

One of Bellabaci's standout features is their emphasis on education. Each set includes access to video tutorials and written guides. They also offer professional training courses for estheticians.

## Pricing

Bellabaci sets are mid-range to premium priced. Their basic starter kit is affordable, while the complete treatment system represents a significant investment.

## Verdict

Bellabaci is a solid choice for both beginners and professionals. The cup quality is excellent, and the educational resources make it easier to learn proper technique. If you want a brand with proven track record in the facial cupping space, Bellabaci is worth considering.
