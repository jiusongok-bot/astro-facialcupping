We gathered perspectives from different types of practitioners who work with facial cupping.

![Medical expert consultation](/images/articles/doctor-smiling.jpg)

## Dermatologists

Most dermatologists view facial cupping as a low-risk complementary practice. Many note that improved circulation from any gentle massage technique can benefit skin health.

## Estheticians

Facial estheticians who incorporate cupping report positive client feedback, particularly for reducing puffiness and creating a more sculpted appearance.

## Traditional Practitioners

Practitioners of Traditional Chinese Medicine see facial cupping as a natural extension of body cupping, consistent with principles of promoting qi flow and blood circulation.
