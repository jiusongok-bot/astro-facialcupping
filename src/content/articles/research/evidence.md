We review the available clinical evidence for cupping therapy, with a focus on applicability to facial cupping.

![Clinical research and devices](/images/articles/doctor-device.jpg)

## Cupping Therapy Research

Systematic reviews of cupping therapy have found evidence for pain reduction and improved local blood flow. However, study quality is often limited.

## Facial-Specific Research

High-quality randomized controlled trials specifically examining facial cupping for cosmetic outcomes are scarce. Most evidence is anecdotal or extrapolated from body cupping studies.

## Our Assessment

The evidence is promising but not conclusive. Facial cupping appears safe when practiced correctly, with many positive anecdotal reports.
