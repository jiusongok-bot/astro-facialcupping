Here we summarize key published studies relevant to cupping therapy and its potential facial applications.

## Cupping and Circulation

Several studies have demonstrated increased local blood flow following cupping therapy using laser Doppler imaging.

## Cupping and Pain

Systematic reviews have found moderate evidence for cupping in managing chronic pain conditions.

## Limitations

Most studies examine body cupping with stronger suction than facial cupping uses. Direct application of these findings to facial cosmetic outcomes requires caution.
