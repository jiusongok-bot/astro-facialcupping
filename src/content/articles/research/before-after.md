Before and after photos are one of the most compelling ways to evaluate facial cupping results.

## What to Look For

Genuine before and after photos should have consistent lighting, angles, and no filters. Results from 4-8 weeks of consistent practice are most meaningful.

## Common Reported Changes

- Reduced puffiness, particularly in the morning
- More defined jawline
- Improved skin radiance
- Subtle lifting effect around the cheekbones

## Important Context

Results vary significantly between individuals. Factors including age, skin type, consistency of practice, and overall health all influence outcomes.
