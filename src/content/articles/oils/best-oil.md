The right oil can make or break your facial cupping experience. We evaluated the most popular facial cupping oils based on glide performance, skin compatibility, and ingredient quality.

## Our Top Picks

**Best Overall: Jojoba Oil** — Closely mimics the skin's natural sebum, provides consistent glide, and works well across all skin types. Its molecular structure is similar to collagen, allowing it to penetrate without clogging pores.

**Best for Dry Skin: Marula Oil** — High in antioxidants and essential fatty acids. Provides long-lasting slip without needing frequent reapplication. Rich in vitamin C and E.

**Best for Oily Skin: Grapeseed Oil** — Featherlight texture that won't feel heavy. Has mild astringent properties that help balance oil production while providing enough slip for cupping.

**Best for Sensitive Skin: Squalane** — Not technically an oil but a lipid naturally produced by the skin. Completely non-irritating, fragrance-free, and provides excellent glide.

## How We Evaluated

Each oil was tested for: total glide time before reapplication, absorption rate, comedogenic rating, and ingredient purity. We prioritized single-ingredient oils with no added fragrances, essential oils, or preservatives.
