This is one of the most common questions about facial cupping. The honest answer is nuanced.

![Doctor with cupping device](/images/articles/doctor-device.jpg)

## What Evidence Supports

Cupping therapy in general has been studied for pain management and circulation. Some studies show modest benefits.

## What Evidence Doesn't Yet Support

High-quality, large-scale clinical trials specifically on facial cupping for cosmetic benefits are limited.

## The Bottom Line

Many practitioners and users report positive experiences. However, facial cupping should be viewed as a complementary practice, not a replacement for proven dermatological treatments.
