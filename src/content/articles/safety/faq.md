## Can facial cupping damage skin?

When done correctly with proper technique and light suction, facial cupping should not damage skin. Damage typically results from excessive suction or improper technique.

## Can you do facial cupping every day?

Yes, with light suction and proper technique. Many practitioners recommend daily 5-10 minute sessions.

## Is facial cupping safe during pregnancy?

Consult your healthcare provider. Pregnancy causes hormonal skin changes that may make skin more sensitive.
