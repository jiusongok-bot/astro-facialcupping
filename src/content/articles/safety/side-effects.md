When done correctly, facial cupping has minimal side effects. However, be aware of the following.

![Cupping therapy on skin](/images/articles/cupping-glass.jpg)

## Common (Mild)

- Temporary redness (resolves within minutes)
- Mild tenderness after first few sessions

## Possible (If Done Incorrectly)

- Bruising from too much suction or stationary cups
- Broken capillaries from aggressive technique
- Skin irritation from inadequate lubrication

## When to Stop

If you experience persistent pain, significant bruising, or skin changes, discontinue use and consult a healthcare provider.
