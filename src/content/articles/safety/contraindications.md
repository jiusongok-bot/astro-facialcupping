description: "Who should avoid facial cupping and when to seek professional advice."

Facial cupping is not suitable for everyone. Avoid or seek medical advice first if you have:

## Absolute Contraindications

- Active skin infections or open wounds on the face
- Severe rosacea or eczema flare-ups
- Blood clotting disorders
- Recent facial surgery or procedures

## Relative Contraindications (Consult First)

- Sensitive or reactive skin
- History of broken capillaries
- Pregnancy (hormonal skin changes)
- Use of blood-thinning medications
