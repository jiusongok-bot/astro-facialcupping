We evaluate facial cupping sets based on five criteria that matter most for a safe and effective experience. Below is our framework and the sets that perform best across each dimension.

## Evaluation Criteria

- **Material quality:** Medical-grade silicone construction verified by material labeling. We reject sets that do not disclose their material grade.
- **Cup sizes:** A minimum of three sizes covering large (cheeks/forehead), medium (jawline/temples), and small (under-eye/nose).
- **Suction control:** Cups should allow graduated squeeze-and-release suction rather than all-or-nothing pressure.
- **Ease of cleaning:** Non-porous surfaces, wide enough openings for brush access, and dishwasher-safe material.
- **Clear instructions:** Written or video guidance covering basic techniques, safety precautions, and recommended routines.

## Top Sets

Bellabaci remains the most recognized brand with their medical-grade silicone sets in five sizes, backed by instructional video libraries. Primally Pure offers a smaller four-cup set with organic facial oil included, emphasizing natural skincare integration. For budget-conscious buyers, several generic medical-grade silicone sets on Amazon meet all five criteria at half the price — just verify the material claims before purchasing.

We assess each set independently and do not accept payment for placements.
