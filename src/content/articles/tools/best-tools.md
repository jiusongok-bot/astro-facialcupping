Beyond traditional cupping sets, several tools can enhance your facial cupping practice.

## Individual Cups

Some practitioners prefer purchasing individual cups in specific sizes rather than sets.

## Electric Cupping Devices

A newer category of devices that automate the suction process. These can be useful for consistent pressure but tend to be more expensive.

## Gua Sha + Cupping Combos

Some practitioners combine facial cupping with gua sha for a more comprehensive treatment.
