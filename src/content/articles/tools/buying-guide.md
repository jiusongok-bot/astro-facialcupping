Before buying a facial cupping set, consider these factors.

![Cupping equipment on table](/images/articles/cupping-equipment.jpg)

## Material

Medical-grade silicone is the standard. Avoid sets that don't specify material grade.

## Cup Sizes

A good set includes at least 3 sizes: large for cheeks and forehead, medium for jawline, small for under-eye and nose area.

## Price Range

Quality facial cupping sets typically range from $15-$45. Extremely cheap sets may use lower-quality materials.

## Included Accessories

Look for sets that include a storage case, cleaning brush, and instruction guide.
