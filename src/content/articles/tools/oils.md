The right facial oil is essential for safe and effective cupping. It creates the glide needed to move cups smoothly across the skin.

![Skincare oils and jade roller](/images/articles/jade-roller-skincare.jpg)

## By Skin Type

- **Dry skin**: Rich oils like marula or avocado
- **Oily skin**: Lightweight oils like jojoba or grapeseed
- **Sensitive skin**: Fragrance-free oils like squalane
- **Mature skin**: Rosehip or argan oil

## Application

Apply a generous layer before cupping. You should be able to feel the oil between your fingers. Reapply during the session if cups start to drag.
