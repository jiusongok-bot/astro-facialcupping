Silicone has become the standard material for facial cupping cups, replacing traditional glass and bamboo.

## Why Silicone

- Soft and flexible for facial contours
- Easy to control suction level
- Durable and easy to clean
- Safe for sensitive skin (medical-grade)
- More affordable than glass alternatives

## What to Look For

Ensure cups are made from medical-grade or food-grade silicone. Avoid cups with strong chemical odors, which may indicate lower-quality materials.
