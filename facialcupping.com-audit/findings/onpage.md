# On-Page SEO Analysis

## Score: 80/100

### What Works
- 11 section list pages with unique, keyword-optimized title tags
- Unique meta descriptions per section
- H1 headings on every page matching section titles
- Clean semantic HTML5 structure (nav, main, header, footer)
- Breadcrumb navigation on every list page
- Article cards using H3 for each entry in grid layout
- Correct canonical URLs on all pages

### Findings

| # | Issue | Severity | Details |
|---|-------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Article titles may exceed 60 chars | Medium | Some article titles are long for SERP display. Review for optimal length. |
| 2 | Missing article OG meta tags | Low | BaseLayout uses og:type='website' only. Article pages should use og:type='article' with published_time and author. |
| 3 | Limited cross-linking between sections | Medium | List pages lack 'Related Sections' widgets to interlink complementary topics. |
| 4 | Title format inconsistency | Low | Some pages append '| FacialCupping.com' differently. Standardize format. |
